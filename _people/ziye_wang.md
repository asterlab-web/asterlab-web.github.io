## Biography

Ziye is an undergraduate majoring in Bioinformatics and Mathematics at Peking University. He is completing his undergraduate thesis in Aster Lab, where he works on coalescent-aware estimation of species tree branch lengths directly from sequence alignments.  

Previously, he has worked on gene drives with [Prof. Jackson Champer](https://jchamper.github.io/), Bayesian phylogenetics with [Prof. Chi Zhang](https://sites.google.com/view/zhangchicool/home), and ancestral recombination graphs with [Prof. Rasmus Nielsen](https://nielsen-lab.github.io/). He is broadly interested in evolutionary genomics, with addtitional interests in Bayesian statistics and stochastic processes in population genetics.
